Social Media Sentiment Analysis

Dataset: 50K+ social media posts
Tools: Python, NLP, VADER
Duration: 6 weeks

Project Overview

Analyzed customer sentiment from social media posts to understand brand perception and identify product improvement opportunities. The project processed over 50,000 posts from Twitter, Reddit, and product review sites using natural language processing techniques.

Business Context

The company launched a new product line but lacked systematic insight into customer sentiment. Traditional surveys provided limited feedback, while thousands of organic customer comments remained unanalyzed. The goal was to extract actionable insights from this unstructured data.

Methodology

  • Data collection via APIs and web scraping (Twitter API, Reddit API)
  • Text preprocessing: cleaning, tokenization, lemmatization
  • Sentiment scoring using VADER (Valence Aware Dictionary and sEntiment Reasoner)
  • Topic modeling with Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA)
  • Time series analysis of sentiment trends
  • Aspect-based sentiment analysis for specific product features

Key Findings

The analysis revealed important insights about customer perception:

  • Overall Sentiment: 52% positive, 31% neutral, 17% negative
  • Peak Negativity: Spike in negative sentiment 2 weeks post-launch due to shipping delays
  • Product Quality: 78% positive mentions about build quality
  • Customer Service: Most negative comments (65%) related to support response time
  • Feature Requests: Battery life mentioned in 1,200+ posts as improvement area
  • Competitor Comparison: Brand viewed more favorably on design but lagged on features

Actionable Recommendations

Based on the analysis, three priority actions were identified:

  • Improve Customer Support Response Time: Implement chatbot for common queries and expand support team to reduce wait times from 48 hours to 4 hours
  • Enhance Battery Performance: Prioritize battery optimization in next software update, communicate improvement timeline to customers
  • Address Shipping Issues: Partner with alternative logistics providers and provide proactive shipping updates

Business Impact

  • Recommendations implemented within 6 weeks of presentation
  • Customer satisfaction score improved from 3.2 to 4.1 stars (28% increase)
  • Negative sentiment decreased by 40% after addressing key issues
  • Support ticket volume reduced by 35% after chatbot deployment
  • Analysis framework adopted for ongoing monthly monitoring

Technical Challenges

Handling sarcasm and context-dependent sentiment proved challenging. For example, "Great, now it won't turn on" tested as positive due to the word "great" but was clearly negative. This was addressed by implementing custom rules and training a more sophisticated model for ambiguous cases.
